Who we are

Honest cars, honest paperwork

VOZIDEX started in 2016 as a small yard in Žižkov with one promise — no pushy sales, no hidden history. Owner Tomáš Novák personally inspects every car that comes through the gate, so you only ever see stock we'd put our own family in.

Today we sell trusted used cars, run a small self-drive and chauffeur rental fleet, and fit accessories in our own workshop. Whatever you drive away in, the paperwork is clean — and the number you call after the sale is still ours.
